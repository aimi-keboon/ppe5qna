About Bandar Springhill

Persatuan Penduduk E5 QNA proudly serves the vibrant community of Bandar Springhill, a well-planned township located in Port Dickson District, Negeri Sembilan. Conceived in July 1997, our neighborhood is part of the Malaysia Vision Valley growth corridor and is strategically positioned near Lukut, adjacent to Bandar Sri Sendayan.

Our Strategic Location

QNA Residence 5, Jalan Springhill, 71010 Port Dickson, Negeri Sembilan

Built on the Lukut Interchange of Seremban-Port Dickson highway, our community is perfectly positioned with only 15 minutes drive to Port Dickson and 12 minutes to Seremban. The Seremban-Port Dickson highway conveniently links us to the North-South Highway.

Our 2,000-acre freehold township offers excellent connectivity, healthcare facilities including UCSI Hospital, educational opportunities with UCSI International School, and is part of a comprehensive development that includes residential, commercial, and recreational components.
