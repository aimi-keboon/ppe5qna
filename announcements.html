AJK Announcements

January 15, 2025
Annual General Meeting (AGM) 2025
Join us for our Annual General Meeting on February 20, 2025, at 7:00 PM at the Community Hall. We will discuss the year's achievements, upcoming projects, and elect new committee members. Light refreshments will be served.
January 10, 2025
New Security Measures
Enhanced security patrols will begin next week. The patrol schedule will be from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM daily. Residents are reminded to ensure their gates are properly locked and to report any suspicious activities to the security team immediately.
January 5, 2025
Community Garden Project
We're excited to announce the launch of our community garden project! Interested residents can register for plot allocations. The garden will feature organic vegetables and herbs. Registration forms are available at the management office.
December 28, 2024
Parking Reminder
Please ensure vehicles are parked only in designated areas. Vehicles parked in fire escape routes or blocking common passages will be towed at the owner's expense. Thank you for your cooperation in maintaining clear access ways.
December 20, 2024
Year-End Appreciation
Thank you to all residents who participated in our community activities throughout 2024. Your active involvement has made our neighborhood a better place to live. We look forward to another great year ahead with more exciting programs and initiatives.
